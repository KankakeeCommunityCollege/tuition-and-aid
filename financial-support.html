Current Funds Available

JTED - Job Training Economic Development
ARPA - American Rescue Plan Act
Fast Track - EV Battery Manufacturing Fast Track Training Support
Students in the following programs may quality for JTED, ARPA and/or Fast Track:

  • Information Technology
  • Diesel Mechanic
  • Automotive Technology
  • Electrical Engineering Technology/Renewable Energy
  • Manufacturing Technology
  • Welding Technology.

PATH - Pipeline for the Advancement of the Healthcare Workforce Program

  • Health Careers students can benefit from this grant.

WIOA - Workforce Innovation & Opportunity Act

  • WIOA is for students in most career and technical programs (includes health careers, technology, technical education and more).
